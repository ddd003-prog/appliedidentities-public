How We Work

From Assessment to Architecture

Every engagement starts with understanding where you actually are — not where an engagement model says you should be.

Discovery Assessment

A structured working session — typically two to three conversations — designed to understand your organization well enough to show you where the identity gaps are and what it would take to close them.

You walk away with a clear picture of AI readiness, an honest assessment of what Identity Architecture would address, and enough information to decide whether deeper work makes sense. Clients consistently tell us the Assessment is valuable in its own right. That is intentional — the first step should not feel like a sales process.

This applies equally to organizations building a foundation before they scale and to organizations already running AI systems that lack one.

The Identity Architecture

Five components, each a governed document your AI systems run on:

Character — The principles that hold under pressure. What wins when values conflict. Delivered as your Company Constitution — the root document every other specification traces back to.

Voice — The reasoning behind how your organization communicates. Not tone — logic. Delivered as Agent Soul Documents — behavioral specifications calibrated by function.

Judgment — The decision patterns your best people execute instinctively. Delivered as your Human Guidebook and Interaction Protocols.

Relationship Context — What different relationships mean and how they are handled. Integrated across all documents in the architecture.

Institutional Memory — Knowledge that exists nowhere on paper — surfaced before it becomes the gap. Captured through the Evaluation Harness and Ground Truth baseline.

What Makes These Documents Different

Machine-readable, not just human-readable. Your AI systems consume these documents directly — they are not strategy decks that sit on a shelf.

Verified and chain-linked. Every document carries embedded verification. The chain traces from any specification back to your approved Company Constitution.

Entirely yours. No proprietary platform dependency. No subscription. Your team or a successor firm can maintain, extend, and verify everything without us.

Governance That Persists

Building the architecture is the first engagement. Knowing it still works is where the value compounds.

Ground Truth Assessment establishes a measurable baseline — where your organization stands across the dimensions that determine how effectively AI can represent you.

The Heartbeat is a structured comparison between your agents’ actual behavior and the identity foundation they were built on — with severity classification and remediation paths when drift is detected.

The Engagement Path

Discovery Assessment — Understand the gaps. Walk away with a clear readiness picture.

Identity Architecture — Build the five-component foundation. Governed documents your AI systems run on.

Ongoing Advisory — Quarterly Heartbeats. Drift detection. Evolution guidance as your deployment matures.
