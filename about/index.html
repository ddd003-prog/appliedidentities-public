About

A Practice, Not a Vendor

Applied Identities was built around a specific premise: the most consequential AI challenge most organizations face is not technical. It is the gap between what the technology can do and what the organization actually needs it to do — a gap that most platform vendors and large consulting firms have little incentive to name, because naming it clearly means slowing down a deployment.

We maintain three to four active client relationships at a time. The problems we address require depth that dilutes when you spread it across a portfolio.

Large consulting firms bring scale. Platform vendors bring capability. Neither focuses on the translation layer — making AI actually reflect who your organization is. That is the gap we address, and we maintain it deliberately.

We deliver strategy that compiles into working infrastructure — governed documents your AI systems consume directly. Implementation is handled by your team or technology partners who specialize in deployment.

Our Credential Is the Work

We run our own Identity Architecture on our own practice. Our Company Constitution is signed and verified. Our agents are governed through the same infrastructure we deliver to clients. The verification registry is public.

We are happy to let the work speak first.
